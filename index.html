Sleep Tracker App Prototype

Project for COGS 3

Previous Next

For this project, I was assigned to design a health app of any kind with a few screens and basic framework between pages. After brainstorming potential different themes for the app, I finally landed on a sleep tracker. Afterwards, I thought about what visuals would be effective for this app. Since people are likely to open an app like this before bed or after they wake up, I decided to make the colors darker and easier on the eyes. Then, I decided which pages would be most crucial to add to the navigation bar at the bottom. I also made sure to implement complementary colors to bring attention to certain elements, such as the current date or buttons that lead you to another page.
